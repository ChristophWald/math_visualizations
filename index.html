Basics: Real sinusoids

Sinusoids are derived from circular motion.

Almost all sounds can be synthesized by adding sinusoids.

Sinusoids have the parameters amplitude, frequency, phase, and offset.

Offset has an indirect effect in multiplication.

Offset can be replaced by adding a cosine with frequency = 0.

Phase has an indirect effect in addition.

Phase shift can be replaced by adding a sine and a cosine.

More Basics: Complex sinusoids

Complex numbers have two coordinate types.

The polar coordinates have two forms.

Multiplying complex numbers is adding angles.

Real sinusoids are sums and differences of complex sinusoids.

Mutliplicating real sinusoids produces two sinusoids with sum/difference frequencies!
